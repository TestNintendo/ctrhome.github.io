Cut the Rope: Triple Treat

Release Date: March 28th, 2014

Platforms:

This three-in-one compilation comprises the original hit, Cut the Rope, along with its sequels, Cut the Rope: Experiments and Cut the Rope: Time Travel. Across more than 650 levels, you’re faced with the task of landing a sugary treat into Om Nom’s mouth by cutting increasingly elaborate rope arrays. You’ll need to plan your approach, and ensure good timing to get the elusive sweetie down to Om Nom so he can scoff it.
