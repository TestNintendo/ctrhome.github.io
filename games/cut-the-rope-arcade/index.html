Cut the Rope Arcade

Release Date: 2016

Platforms: Arcade (Windows-Based)


Based on the popular app, Cut the Rope is a single player game where the player has to figure out the best way to get the candy to the small alien character 'Om Nom'. The player has to cut a series of ropes along with timing other features to win the game. Along with its striking touch screen, the cabient is a stand-out piece that reflects the game and makes it a feature in any location.
